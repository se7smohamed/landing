Welcome Message

Dear Colleagues and Friends, It gives me a great pleasure to invite you on behalf of the congress board and the local organizing committee to attend The first Egypt and Pan Arab Congress of Musculoskeletal, Neuromuscular and Spine Ultrasound. This meeting will take place in Cairo, Egypt, from 1-2 December 2021. This congress was planned to be held last year June 2020, but due to the worldwide circumstances of COVID-19 pandemic, it was unfortunately postponed. This year it will be Hybrid Meeting, giving the chance for our eminent international speakers to participate virtually and for attendees to attend the congress online too. Side by side we will welcome speakers and attendees face to face. We are confident that we will be able to make this congress a very special and memorable event. The scientific sessions will be dominated by outstanding speakers from Egypt, Arab and International medical communities. The congress will be suitable for beginners to learn and grow their expertise and for senior colleagues to keep up to date. Different specialties will be concerned including Rheumatology. Physical Medicine, Orthopedics, Plastic and Microsurgery, Pain Management, Radiology. Neurology and Neurosurgery. ESMNS together with the Arab League will offer you the chance to network with the ultrasound professionals from around the globe, whilst experiencing the vibrant culture, gastronomy, history and architecture of Cairo for our friends and colleagues who will be able to attend face to face. This congress is a timely reminder of the inter-related nature of today's disease management and is a live experience of teamwork and collaboration on various scales. Tinally, the Organizing Committee is diligently working to present a remarkable fascinating program to ensure a successful meeting outcome. We are looking forward to welcome you to Cairo 2021.

Dr. Adham Aboul Fotouh Khalil
EULAR Certified MSUS Trainer
